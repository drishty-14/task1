After a year's preparation,it was time to go backpacking with the Boy Scouts. We flew
to Colorado Springs,toured the Air Force Acadamy,Pike's Peak,Garden Of The Gods,

Amtrack's and rode the rails to Kingman,and then chartered a bus home.
